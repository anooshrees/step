Movie Recommendation Chatbot (February-March 2020)

Implemented sentiment extraction and item-item collaborative filtering in Python in order to construct a fully-functional chatbot that could provide movie recommendations based on a user's previous preferences, in addition to responding to emotions and carrying on basic conversation. The chatbot could also perform spellcheck, correct for ambiguous response, and fail gracefully if provided with nonsensical/irrelevant content.

A Comparative Analysis of Hate Speech on Social Media Platforms (October-December 2019)

In order to identify extremists increasingly using social networks to broadcast hate speech and advertize violence, I used three models to analyze three datasets from Twitter, Reddit, and a white supremacist platform and perform one-versus-the-rest multiclass classification after pre-processing with Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERT). After training and testing our models, I also compared the types of hate speech prevalent on each site using unsupervised clustering. Through feature analysis and comparitive analysis, I ultimately identified defining features of hate speech detectors, sources of bias, and methodology useful for flagging possibly dangerous accounts.

Multilingual Reseller Model Expansion (June-September 2019)

Training data for the Google Ads Integrity reseller model is dominated by English examples; in order to improve our performance in Russian, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Polish, and Turkish (languages belonging to countries with sensitive laws and limitations), I designed a more effecient preprocessing method for the training data (composed of HTML landing pages) and translated each example with an internal API. I then completed feature extraction and analysis of feature statistics in order to prep the training set for use with a multilingual model.

A Map-Building, Self-Driving, Voice-Controlled Service Robot (June 2016-May 2017)

Built and programmed autonomous robot with rich sensor suite and software stack in Python running on ROS. I also implemented capabilities to map an indoor environment and navigate to a destination on voice command, with the intention of helping those with impaired vision navigate their households and public spaces. Ultimately, the final prototype successfully aided a family member with poor vision around his home.
